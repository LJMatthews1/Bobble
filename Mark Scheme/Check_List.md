# Check List:

**Goal:** Ensure every page is highly engaging, user-centered, and effectively communicates the value proposition.

## 1. Core Messaging & Value (The "Hook")
* [ ] **Instant Clarity:** Does the top of the page immediately explain Bobble's value?
* [ ] **Benefit Focus:** Is the copy about "What the user gets" rather than "What Bobble does"?
* [ ] **USP Presence:** Is the unique "Hobby Pass" or "Credit System" logic clear on this page?
* [ ] **Consistent Brand:** Does the tone feel playful, community-focused, and accessible?

## 2. Professional UX/UI (The "Look")
* [ ] **High-Fidelity Visuals:** Are all images crisp, consistent, and professional?
* [ ] **The 3-Click Rule:** Can users reach their desired goal (e.g., booking/signing up) in 3 clicks?
* [ ] **Visual Hierarchy:** Is the most important action (CTA) the most prominent element?
* [ ] **Responsive Design:** Is the layout fluid and perfectly usable on both mobile and desktop?
* [ ] **Legibility:** Is text high-contrast and are tap targets (buttons) appropriately sized?

## 3. Engagement & Interactivity (The "Stickiness")
* [ ] **Active Elements:** Does the page have interactive features? (e.g., hover effects, filters, quizzes, or calculators).
* [ ] **Primary CTAs:** Is there a clear "Join," "Book," or "Search" button visible without scrolling?
* [ ] **Secondary Capture:** Is there a way to capture "lukewarm" users? (e.g., waitlists or newsletter signups).
* [ ] **System Feedback:** Do buttons and links provide immediate visual feedback when clicked?

## 4. Business Credibility (The "Trust")
* [ ] **Social Proof:** Are there mentions of research data or traction? (e.g., "Join 500+ Londoners").
* [ ] **Target Alignment:** Does the imagery and content specifically appeal to the London 20-45 demographic?
* [ ] **Trust Signals:** Are there visible "Secure," "Verified," or "FAQ" elements?
* [ ] **Research Link:** Is the content clearly derived from your primary/secondary market research?
